Why Hewlett Packard Enterprise (HPE) International Revenue Trends Deserve Your Attention

2025-09-08 13:15:02+00:00 UTC
  • Of the total revenue, $1.65 billion came from Asia Pacific and Japan during the last fiscal quarter, accounting for 18.1%.
  • This represented a surprise of -8.13% as analysts had expected the region to contribute $1.8 billion to the total revenue.
  • The recent quarter saw the company's total revenue reaching $9.14 billion, marking an improvement of 18.5% from the prior-year quarter.
  • When compared to the consensus estimate of $3.07 billion, this meant a surprise of -10.87%.
  • In comparison, the region contributed $1.59 billion, or 20.8%, and $1.75 billion, or 22.6%, to total revenue in the previous and year-ago quarters, respectively.
  • A Dive into HPE's International Revenue Trends During the quarter, Europe, Middle East and Africa contributed $2.74 billion in revenue, making up 30% of the total revenue.
  • Europe, Middle East and Africa and Asia Pacific and Japan are expected to contribute 35.2% (translating to $3.52 billion), and 20.6% ($2.06 billion) to the total revenue, respectively.
