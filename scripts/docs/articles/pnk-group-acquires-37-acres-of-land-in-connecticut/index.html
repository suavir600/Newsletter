PNK Group Acquires 37 Acres of Land in Connecticut

2025-09-15 12:43:00+00:00 UTC
  • 15, 2025 /PRNewswire/ -- Industrial developer PNK Group has acquired the Baker Hollow site in Windsor, CT.
  • In March 2025, the company acquired a 21.75-acre site for the development of a 218,000-square-foot industrial facility.
  • Located just two miles from Interstate 91, ensuring excellent connectivity for the project.
  • The 37-acre property will be developed into two facilities of 300,000 and 120,000 square feet.
  • Windsor is a part of the Hartford metropolitan area, which has a population of over 1.2 million people.
  • The PNK Baker Hollow site is conveniently located, providing access to over 44 million people across New England, New York, and New Jersey.
