Intel Stock Rises After Chipmaker Cuts Forecast for Expenses

2025-09-15T14:10:42+00:00
  • Intel added that it would retain control of the remaining 49% of Altera.
  • In a regulatory filing, it also noted that its target of fiscal 2026 operating expenses remained unchanged at $16.0 billion.
  • The company had disclosed the sale of 51% of Altera to private equity firm Silver Lake for approximately $3.3 billion in April, and the deal closed last Friday.
  • Because of the infusion of cash, Intel now has a fiscal 2025 non-GAAP operating expenses target of $16.8 billion, down from its previous guidance of $17.0 billion.
