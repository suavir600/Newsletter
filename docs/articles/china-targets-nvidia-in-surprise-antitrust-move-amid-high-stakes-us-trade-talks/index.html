China Targets Nvidia in Surprise Antitrust Move Amid High-Stakes US Trade Talks

2025-09-15 12:35:02+00:00
  • Nvidia's stock slipped about 2% in pre-market trading, with Texas Instruments also down by a similar margin, as US index futures gave back early gains.
  • Beijing had originally cleared Nvidia's $7 billion Mellanox deal four years ago, but only on the condition that Chinese firms wouldn't be shut out.
  • Regulators in Beijing said the company's 2020 Mellanox acquisition ran afoul of anti-monopoly rules, a move landing squarely as US and Chinese officials press through their second day of tariff talks in Madrid.
  • The timing isn't accidental.
  • This article first appeared on GuruFocus.
  • And the backdrop is only getting more complicated.
  • China just pulled Nvidia (NASDAQ:NVDA) deeper into the trade crossfire.
