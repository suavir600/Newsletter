Why Labubu maker Pop Mart may see its stock bubble burst

  • Yin expects overseas markets to contribute more than 60% of earnings by 2027, with Labubu alone making up 35% of sales.
  • However, it sank over 6% on Monday in Hong Kong trading and dropped 10% over the past five trading days.

  • The company is trading at a rich 31 times estimated 2025 P/E and 22 times 2026 P/E — multiples that leave little margin for error.
