Is Costco's 5.7% Q4 Comparable Sales Growth a Bullish Signal?

2025-09-15T14:09:00+00:00
  • We note that net sales for the quarter reached $84.4 billion, up 8% from $78.2 billion last year.
  • Costco Wholesale Corporation COST reported a 5.7% rise in comparable sales for the fourth quarter of fiscal 2025, covering the 16 weeks ended Aug.
  • comparable sales were up 5.1%, while Canada rose 6.3%, and Other International markets surged 8.6%.
  • comparable sales rising 4.6% in the second quarter of fiscal 2026, supported by strength in grocery, health & wellness and improving trends in general merchandise.
  • While second-quarter sales dipped 0.9% year over year, in-store traffic trends improved significantly.
  • Digital channels again proved to be a strong growth driver, with e-commerce comparable sales climbing 13.6% in the quarter.
  • When excluding the impacts of changes in gasoline prices and foreign exchange, total company comparable sales advanced 6.4%.
