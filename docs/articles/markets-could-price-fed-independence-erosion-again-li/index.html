Markets Could Price Fed Independence Erosion Again: Li

2025-09-08T09:20:26+00:00
  • Li speaks on Bloomberg Television.
  • BlackRock's Wei Li says the cooling down of the US jobs market creates "enough macro justification for the Fed to go ahead with near-term rate cuts" without the central bank's independence being brought into question.
  • She also adds that markets could start to price back in some of the "previous independence erosion premia," which can have a positive effect on equities and bonds.
