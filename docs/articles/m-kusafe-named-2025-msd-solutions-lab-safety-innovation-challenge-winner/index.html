MākuSafe Named 2025 MSD Solutions Lab Safety Innovation Challenge Winner

2025-09-19T19:00:00+00:00
  • Held during the 2025 NSC Safety Congress & Expo, MākuSafe was selected as this year's winner.
  • 19, 2025 /PRNewswire/ -- The National Safety Council showcased 10 breakthrough technologies to help prevent musculoskeletal disorders (MSDs) at the fourth annual MSD Solutions Lab Safety Innovation Challenge.
  • Connect with NSC: Facebook Twitter LinkedIn YouTube Instagram ©2025 National Safety Council Cision View original content to download multimedia: https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/mkusafe-named-2025-msd-solutions-lab-safety-innovation-challenge-winner-302561828.html
  • Congratulations to all our semifinalists and especially to MākuSafe for being named this year's winner." Launched by the NSC MSD Solutions Lab , established in 2021 with funding from Amazon (NASDAQ: AMZN), the Safety Innovation Challenge highlights technologies that address MSD risk factors like repetitive motion, awkward posture and overexertion.
  • The 2025 semifinalists include: CerebrumEdge : AI-powered computer vision platform using cameras to monitor worker posture, detect early MSD risks and provide prevention insights SpatialCortex : Wearable sensor and AI-enabled system delivering biomechanical risk analysis, manual-handling assessments and task-specific 3D training KU Leuven : Digital twin technology replicating workplaces and workers allowing for proactive analysis of postures, movements and potential risks…
  • About the National Safety Council The National Safety Council is America's leading nonprofit safety advocate – and has been for over 110 years.
