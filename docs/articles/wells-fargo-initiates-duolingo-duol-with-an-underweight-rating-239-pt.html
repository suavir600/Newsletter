Wells Fargo Initiates Duolingo (DUOL) with an Underweight Rating, $239 PT

  • Duolingo reported an EPS of $0.91, which exceeded the expected $0.55.
  • On September 8, Wells Fargo initiated coverage of Duolingo with an Underweight rating and $239 price target.
  • The company offers courses in 40 different languages through its Duolingo app.
  • The firm believes Duolingo’s user growth issues will persist in the medium term, and also believes that the company’s 2027 estimates have downside risk.
  • The company announced a strong performance, with sales increasing to $252 million from $178 million year-over-year, and net income growing to $45 million from $24 million in the same period.
  • (NASDAQ:DUOL) is one of the best up and coming stocks with huge upside potential.
  • Due to this strong performance, the company is once again raising its full-year guidance.
