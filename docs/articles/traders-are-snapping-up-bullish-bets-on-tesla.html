Traders Are Snapping Up Bullish Bets on Tesla

  • Options traders are piling into bullish bets on Tesla after Elon Musk disclosed a $1 billion purchase of the EV-maker's stock.
  • Some of the most actively-traded options contracts were Tesla call options that would pay out if shares jump to $430 or $450 apiece, according to Cboe Global Markets data.
