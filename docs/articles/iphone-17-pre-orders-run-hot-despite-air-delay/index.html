iPhone 17 Pre-Orders Run Hot Despite Air Delay

2025-09-15 21:19:47+00:00 UTC
  • Wedbush's Dan Ives thinks pre-orders could run 510% above last year.
  • Shares nudged 1% higher in premarket trading as Wall Street cheered signs of a healthy upgrade cycle.
  • Wedbush kept its Outperform rating and $270 target.
  • He points out that nearly 20% of Apple's 1.5 billion users haven't upgraded in more than 4 years, calling it a massive opportunity for Tim Cook and team to spark another wave of iPhone sales.
  • Apple analyst Ming-Chi Kuo also sees stronger demand than last year's iPhone 16 launch, saying it could give Apple's Q3 results a lift.
  • Kuo added the Air is a phone that has to be seen in person to appreciate the kind of device that might win you over once it's in your hand, even at $999.
  • Apple's ( NASDAQ:AAPL ) iPhone 17 is off to a hot start , with pre-orders climbing this week and analysts saying the buzz is real.
