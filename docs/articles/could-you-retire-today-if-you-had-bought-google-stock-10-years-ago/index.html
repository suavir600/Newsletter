Could You Retire Today If You Had Bought Google Stock 10 Years Ago?

2025-09-18T11:57:06+00:00
  • As of late 2025, Alphabet trades around $251.
  • In late 2015, Google stock (GOOGL) closed around $37 (split-adjusted).
  • Someone who invested $2,000 in Google in 2015 would have about $13,400 today.
  • To reach that from Google stock alone, you would have needed to invest roughly $150,000 in 2015.
  • If you need $60,000 annually, you’d need about $1,500,000 saved, requiring about $225,000 invested in Google in 2015.
  • Today, it’s worth about $251 per share.
  • Most people don’t have $100,000+ sitting around to invest in a single stock.
