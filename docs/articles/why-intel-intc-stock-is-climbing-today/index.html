Why Intel (INTC) Stock Is Climbing Today

2025-09-15 20:08:37+00:00 UTC
  • Guidance for 2026 remains unchanged at $16.0 billion.
  • With shares already up about 25% in 2025, the company's sharper focus on cost control adds momentum to its turnaround story.
  • The cash boost allows Intel to lower its 2025 non-GAAP operating expenses forecast to $16.8 billion from $17.0 billion.
  • The company closed its $3.3 billion transaction with Silver Lake last Friday, transferring 51% of Altera while retaining the remaining 49%.
  • Shares climbed more than 4% after the opening bell, adding to a strong year-to-date performance.
