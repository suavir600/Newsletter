Should Investors Add the Invesco QQQ Trust to Their Core Holdings?

2025-09-08T09:08:00+00:00
  • For comparison, the S&P 500's technology weighting is around 34%, while tech makes up nearly 61% of the Invesco QQQ Trust's portfolio.
  • The top 10 of the S&P 500 are also tech heavy, but the list is only about 38% of the portfolio.
  • Worse, the top 10 holdings in the Invesco QQQ Trust account for nearly 53% of the portfolio's assets.
  • Even a technology-focused ETF like The Technology Select Sector SPDR Fund has an expense ratio of just 0.08%.
  • One interesting fact here is that the Invesco QQQ Trust's expense ratio is on the high side for an index ETF, at 0.20%.
  • To put some numbers on that, the expense ratio is 0.09% for the SPDR S&P 500 ETF and 0.03% for the Vanguard S&P 500 ETF.
  • In this case, the index is the Nasdaq 100 index.
