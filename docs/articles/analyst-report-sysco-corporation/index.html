Analyst Report: Sysco Corporation

2025-09-08 08:10:08+00:00
  • Sysco is the largest US foodservice distributor with 17% share of the highly fragmented $370 billion domestic market.
  • In fiscal 2025, 70% of the firm’s revenue was derived from its US foodservice operations, while its international (18%), quick-service logistics (10%), and other (2%) segments contributed the rest.
