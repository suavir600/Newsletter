Musk Invests $1B in Tesla, Sending Shares Soaring

2025-09-15T15:49:28+00:00
  • Tesla shares climbed 7.4% Friday and were up another 6% in premarket Monday, trading at $419.50.
  • A new SEC filing shows Musk picked up 2,568,732 shares on September 12, paying between $371.38 and $396.54 each.
  • Shareholders will get their say on the proposal at the annual meeting on November 6.
  • The plan vests in 12 stages and requires him to stay on as CEO for at least 7.5 years.
  • Tesla's board recently approved a new pay package for Musk that could reach $1 trillion if he hits a series of ambitious performance targets.
  • The timing is notable.
  • This article first appeared on GuruFocus.
