Cadence Design Systems (NASDAQ:CDNS) Has A Rock Solid Balance Sheet

2025-09-15 12:00:27+00:00 UTC
  • And it impressed us with free cash flow of US$1.6b, being 95% of its EBIT.
  • As you can see below, at the end of June 2025, Cadence Design Systems had US$2.48b of debt, up from US$1.35b a year ago.
  • So its liabilities outweigh the sum of its cash and (near-term) receivables by US$747.4m.
  • However, its balance sheet shows it holds US$3.00b in cash, so it actually has US$522.8m net cash.
  • Offsetting this, it had US$3.00b in cash and US$753.8m in receivables that were due within 12 months.
  • So it's very unlikely that the US$93.6b company is short on cash, but still worth keeping an eye on the balance sheet.
  • During the last three years, Cadence Design Systems generated free cash flow amounting to a very robust 95% of its EBIT, more than we'd expect.
