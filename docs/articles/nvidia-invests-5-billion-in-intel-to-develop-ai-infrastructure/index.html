Nvidia invests $5 billion in Intel to develop AI infrastructure

2025-09-18T13:06:11+00:00
  • In premarket trading, Intel shares soared 30%, while Nvidia shares rose almost 3%.
  • Nvidia said it will spend $5bn to buy Intel common stock at $23.28 a share.
  • The company fell into a slump after missing the shift to the mobile computing era unleashed by the iPhone's 2007 debut.
  • Nvidia, the world's leading chipmaker, announced on Thursday that it's investing $5 billion (€4.23bn) in Intel and it will collaborate with the struggling semiconductor company on products.
  • The investment is subject to regulatory approvals.
  • For PC products, Intel will build chips that integrate Nvidia technology.
  • For data centres, Intel will make custom chips that Nvidia will use in its AI infrastructure platforms.
