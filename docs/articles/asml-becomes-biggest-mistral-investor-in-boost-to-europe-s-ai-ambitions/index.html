ASML becomes biggest Mistral investor in boost to Europe's AI ambitions

2025-09-09 05:07:38+00:00
  • ASML's investment made it Mistral's main shareholder with a stake of about 11%.
  • ASML's shares were up 1% in early Amsterdam trading, giving it a market value of 268 billion euros.
  • It is also led by French CEO Christophe Fouquet, who took the helm in 2024.
  • Mistral AI raised a total of 1.7 billion euros ($2 billion) in its latest funding round, it said on Tuesday.
  • ($1 = 0.8495 euros) (Reporting by Gianluca Lo Nostro; Editing by Tom Hogue, Jacqueline Wong and Matt Scuffham)
  • Mistral, founded in 2023 by former researchers from Google DeepMind and Meta, has positioned itself as Europe's AI alternative to the U.S.
  • OpenAI is eyeing a valuation of around $500 billion in a potential stock sale, a source familiar with the matter told Reuters in August, more than 40 times Mistral's valuation.
