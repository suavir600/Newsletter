Is Urban Outfitters Set to Extend Its Multi-Brand Momentum in FY26?

2025-09-08 15:33:00+00:00
  • URBN reported record second-quarter fiscal 2026 results, with sales rising 11.3% year over year to $1.5 billion.
  • Free People delivered a 14% increase in revenues, led by a 6.7% retail comparable sales gain and a 19% rise in wholesale revenues.
  • Nuuly achieved its most profitable quarter, posting a 53.2% revenue increase as active subscribers climbed 48.1% to about 370,000.
  • FP Movement continued to fuel momentum with 30% total growth, including 14% retail comps and a 52% wholesale rally.
  • Anthropologie continued its consistent momentum, with 5.7% retail comps, extending more than four years of consecutive quarterly gains.
  • The Urban Outfitters brand delivered 5% revenue growth and 4.2% retail comps, including an 11% increase in Europe and improved results in North America.
  • Proprietary labels, such as BDG denim and Out from Under lounge, grew more than 30% in the past six months, while collaborations with Nike and Chipotle helped attract customers.
