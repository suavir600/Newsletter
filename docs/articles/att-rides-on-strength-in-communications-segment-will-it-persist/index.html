AT&T Rides on Strength in Communications Segment: Will it Persist?

2025-09-08 14:46:00+00:00 UTC
  • During the second quarter, net sales improved 6.7% year over year to $21.84 billion.
  • During the second quarter, the Consumer Wireline business revenues were up 5.8% to $3.54 billion.
  • The consolidated Communication revenues are expected to be $122.3 billion, indicating a 4% year-over-year rise.
  • Per our estimate, from the Consumer Wireline business, the company is expected to generate $15 billion in revenues in 2025, indicating 10.5% year-over-year growth.
  • In the second quarter, Verizon’s Consumer revenues were up 6.9% year over year to $26.65 billion, driven by growth in both services and wireless equipment.
  • The company is also acquiring Lumen's fiber connectivity business for $5.75 billion.
  • T-Mobile’s Equipment revenues were $3.43 billion, up from $3.1 billion in the year-ago quarter.
