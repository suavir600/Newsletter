JPM starts Cognex with Neutral view, sees AI potential but near-term risks

2025-09-15T16:00:50+00:00
  • JPMorgan expects margins to improve over time, with EBITDA rising from about 19% in 2024 to 25% by 2028, supported by sales transformation, operating leverage and R&D efficiency.
  • Cognex currently trades at about 31 times estimated 2026 EBITDA and 39 times earnings, a premium to industrial technology peers.
  • “Upside could be realized if AI adoption scales faster and margins surpass 25%, but customer concentration and intensifying competition present ongoing headwinds,” JPMorgan said.
  • Investing.com -- JPMorgan initiated coverage of Cognex with a Neutral rating and a $45 price target, saying the machine vision company has strong long-term prospects but limited upside at current levels.
