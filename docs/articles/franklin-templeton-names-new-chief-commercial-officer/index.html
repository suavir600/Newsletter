Franklin Templeton names new chief commercial officer

2025-09-09 12:12:58+00:00 UTC
  • Franklin Templeton has appointed Daniel Gamba as its new chief commercial officer, effective 15 October 2025.
  • Gamba brings 25 years of experience to Franklin Templeton, having previously served as president of Northern Trust's asset management business.
  • Gamba succeeds Adam Spector, who has transitioned to the role of CEO at Fiduciary Trust International.
  • Based in New York City, Gamba will be responsible for overseeing global sales, marketing, and product strategy.
  • The trio will collaborate closely with Johnson and the board of directors to support the firm's long-term plans.
  • He will report directly to Jenny Johnson, the CEO of Franklin Templeton, and also join the firm’s executive committee.
  • Prior to that, Gamba's over two-decade stint at BlackRock saw him heading investments, research, distribution, and product teams.
