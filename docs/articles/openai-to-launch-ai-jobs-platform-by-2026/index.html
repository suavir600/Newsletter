OpenAI to Launch AI Jobs Platform by 2026

2025-09-08 21:19:08+00:00
  • The company announced Thursday it's building an AI-driven jobs platform that will connect candidates with employers, with a launch expected by mid-2026.
  • Microsoft has invested $13 billion in OpenAI, but the partnership has gotten more complicated as the two increasingly overlap in search, advertising, and now potentially in career networking.
  • At the same time, OpenAI is beefing up its learning hub, OpenAI Academy.
  • This article first appeared on GuruFocus.
  • OpenAI is taking a direct shot at LinkedIn.
  • Learners will practice through ChatGPT's Study mode.
  • The news came in a blog post from Fidji Simo, OpenAI's chief executive of applications and former Instacart CEO.
