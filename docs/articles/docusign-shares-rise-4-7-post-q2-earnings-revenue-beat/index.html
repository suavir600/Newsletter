DocuSign Shares Rise 4.7% Post Q2 Earnings & Revenue Beat

2025-09-08 14:05:00+00:00
  • Total revenues of $800.6 million beat the consensus mark by 2.8% and rose 8.8% from the second quarter of fiscal 2025.
  • Billings amounted to $818 million, up 13% from the year-ago quarter.
  • The non-GAAP gross profit of $656.9 million grew 8.6% year over year and outpaced our expectation of $630.7 million.
  • DOCU’s Q3 & FY26 Guidance For the third quarter of fiscal 2026, the company expects revenues between $804 million and $808 million.
  • It beat our estimate of 27.3%.
  • For fiscal 2026, the company expects revenues between $3.189 billion and $3.201 billion.
  • Professional services and other revenues of $16.25 million fell 12.9% from the year-ago quarter, beating our expectation of $17.5 million.
