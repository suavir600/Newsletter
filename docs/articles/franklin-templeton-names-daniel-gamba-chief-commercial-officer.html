Franklin Templeton Names Daniel Gamba Chief Commercial Officer

  • Appoints Daniel Gamba, Terrence Murphy and Matthew Nicholls as Co-Presidents SAN MATEO, Calif., September 08, 2025--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Franklin Resources, Inc.
  • (Franklin Templeton; NYSE:BEN) today announced the appointment of Daniel Gamba as Chief Commercial Officer, overseeing global sales, marketing and product strategy, effective October 15, 2025.
  • He will be based in New York City.
  • Throughout his more than 25 years in the asset management business, he has become a trusted partner to clients in all segments across our industry.
  • Franklin Templeton’s regionally focused sales model, which consists of over 1,500 professionals, is driven by a consultative, investment-led mindset, and has contributed greatly to this growth.
  • In addition, the company announced that Gamba, Terrence Murphy, Head of Public Market Investments, and Matthew Nicholls, Chief Financial and Operating Officer, will become Co-Presidents, effective October 15.
  • Gamba will report to Jenny Johnson, Chief Executive Officer of Franklin Templeton, and join the company’s Executive Committee.
