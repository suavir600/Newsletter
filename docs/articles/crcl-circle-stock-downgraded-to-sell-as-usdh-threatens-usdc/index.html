CRCL: Circle Stock Downgraded to 'Sell' as USDH Threatens USDC

2025-09-08 13:08:55+00:00 UTC
  • Sep 8 - Circle Internet Group (CRCL) faces fresh pressure after Compass Point analyst Ed Engel cut his rating to Sell and lowered his price target to $96, a 28% drop.
  • He estimates Hyperliquid's incentive program could strip as much as $100 million from Circle's gross profit if customers migrate.
  • The downgrade indicates high risk, and it is more momentum-driven than execution-driven.
  • This article first appeared on GuruFocus.
  • Circle depends on fees tied to USDC market share, so sustained outflows would hit revenue and margins.
  • Engel says Hyperliquid's planned USDH stablecoin threatens to pull users and revenue from Circle's USDC.
  • Trading and filings will be monitored by market participants to detect evidence of impact early.
