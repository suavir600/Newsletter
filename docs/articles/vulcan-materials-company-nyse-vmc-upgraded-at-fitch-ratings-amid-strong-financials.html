Vulcan Materials Company (NYSE:VMC) Upgraded at Fitch Ratings amid Strong Financials

  • Vulcan Materials has affirmed its strong financial position following the $2.3 billion acquisition in 2024.
  • While the company’s EBITDA margin improved by 200 basis points in 2024, Fitch Ratings expects it to improve by between 100 and 150 basis points in 2025, driven by strong pricing and operational efficiencies.
  • On August 28, the ratings firm upgraded the company’s Long-term Issuer Default Rating to BBB+ from BBB.
