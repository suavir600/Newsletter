Costco Q4 Sales Hit $84.4B: What's Fueling the 8% Revenue Growth?

2025-09-08 12:33:00+00:00
  • Costco Wholesale Corporation COST reported a robust financial performance for its fourth quarter of fiscal 2025 with net sales reaching $84.4 billion, an 8% increase from $78.2 billion last year.
  • Momentum was even stronger in August, when the metric surged 18.4%.
  • These factors combined to deliver an 8.1% increase in net sales of $269.9 billion for the fiscal year despite a challenging economic environment.
  • comparable sales rising 4.6% in the second quarter of fiscal 2026, supported by strength in grocery, health & wellness, and improving trends in general merchandise.
  • While second-quarter sales dipped 0.9% year over year, in-store traffic trends improved significantly.
  • When excluding the impacts of changes in gasoline prices and foreign exchange, total comparable sales rose 6.4%.
  • Canada led with an 8.3% increase, followed by 7.2% growth in other international markets and 6% jump in the U.S market.
