We compared Milwaukee grocery prices with other Wisconsin cities. Here's what we found

2025-09-08 10:04:00+00:00
  • Walmart's basket totaled $54.38 and Meijer's $54.97.
  • The equivalent list of name-brand goods cost $73.37 at Tosa's Meijer.
  • At Pick 'n Save, though, it's a two-pack of Bounty paper towel rolls for $9.49.
  • The full name-brand list will run shoppers $81.53, while the store-brand cost $60.13.
  • At Pick 'n Save, that's a difference of more than $20 if shoppers buy Kroger or Roundy-brand items.
  • Bacon was the most expensive name-brand item at both Walmart ($8.48 per pound pound) and Meijer ($7.89 per pound).
  • Walmart in West Milwaukee emerged as the cheapest of the three, with the full basket of name-brand items totaling $70.19.
