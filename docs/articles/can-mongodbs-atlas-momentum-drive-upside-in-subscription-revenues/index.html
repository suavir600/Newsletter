Can MongoDB's Atlas Momentum Drive Upside in Subscription Revenues?

2025-09-08 15:39:00+00:00 UTC
  • The Zacks Consensus Estimate for the third quarter of fiscal 2026 subscription revenues is pegged at $571.7 million, with the consensus mark for Atlas revenues pegged at $455.8 million, up 25.7% from the year-ago quarter
  • MDB reported 2,564 customers generating at least $100,000 in annual recurring revenues, a 17% increase from the prior year.
  • In the second quarter of fiscal 2026, Atlas revenues increased 29% year over year and represented 74% of MDB’s total revenues.
  • With a net annual recurring revenue expansion rate of nearly 119%, MDB is viewed as demonstrating durable retention alongside consistent workload expansion.
  • The platform was serving more than 58,300 customers at the quarter’s end, up from 49,200 a year earlier, reflecting both new adoption and the expansion of existing deployments.
  • This enterprise orientation has been translating into momentum.
  • Adoption is increasingly concentrated in larger, more durable deployments.
