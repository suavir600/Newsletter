Strategy Adds $60 Million to Bitcoin Treasury in Smallest Buy in a Month

2025-09-15 15:44:17+00:00
  • Strategy’s stock price fell 2% to $325 on Monday, according to Yahoo Finance.
  • In a Myriad market, more than 80% of respondents believe that Bitcoin will trade above $105,000 throughout September.
  • Bitcoin meanwhile changed hands around $115,000, a 0.4% decrease over the past day, according to crypto data provider CoinGecko.
  • The Tysons, Virginia-based firm now owns roughly 639,000 Bitcoin worth $73 billion.
  • Over the period, it bought 585 Bitcoin worth $67 million, with one week representing its lightest activity since late March.
  • Strategy, formerly MicroStrategy, unveiled its smallest Bitcoin purchase in a month on Monday, scooping up 525 Bitcoin worth $60 million, according to a press release.
  • Although the company raised around $68.2 million through its STRF, STRC, and STRD offerings, the company only spent $60.2 million on Bitcoin, leaving it with around $8 million in extra cash.
