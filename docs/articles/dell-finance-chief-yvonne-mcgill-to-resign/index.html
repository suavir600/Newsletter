Dell finance chief Yvonne McGill to resign

2025-09-08T20:20:42+00:00
  • (Reuters) - Dell Technologies said on Monday its Chief Financial Officer Yvonne McGill would resign effective September 9.
  • (Reporting by Savyata Mishra in Bengaluru; Editing by Arun Koyyur)
  • The company also reaffirmed its third-quarter and full-year forecasts.
  • David Kennedy, senior vice president of Global Business Operations, will serve in the interim role, the PC maker said.
