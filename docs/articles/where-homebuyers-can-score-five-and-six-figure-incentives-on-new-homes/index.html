Where homebuyers can score five- and six-figure incentives on new homes

2025-09-15 16:00:00+00:00
  • Century Communities is offering $5,000 toward closing costs as well as a fixed rate below 5%.
  • For buyers with homes closed by March 31, 2026, Beazer is offering a 4.99% fixed mortgage rate.
  • Smith Douglas Homes is offering buyers a 3.99% 5/1 adjustable-rate mortgage with $0 closing costs.
  • PulteGroup is offering buyers a 4.25% mortgage rate on select QMIs as well as half off design options up to $30,000 and 6% toward closing costs.
  • Richmond American Homes has cut prices by over $100,000 on a few homes in The Greens.
  • At Beazer’s Estates by Lake Jessup, buyers can take advantage of 50% off options from the design center, in addition to $20,000 for closing costs.
  • David Weekley Homes is offering 1% back for closing costs, plus $20,000, either as a discount, or for closing costs in its River District-Pioneer Collection neighborhood.
