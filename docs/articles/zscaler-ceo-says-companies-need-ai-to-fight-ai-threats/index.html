Zscaler CEO says companies need AI to fight AI threats

2025-09-15T13:24:29+00:00
  • Zscaler shares are up over 57% year to date, while Palo Alto Networks (PANW) and CrowdStrike (CRWD) have also surged, suggesting that investors are betting AI will drive new demand for advanced security systems.
  • He cited the Colonial Pipeline hack in 2021, which shut down fuel supplies across the East Coast, as an example of how quickly an attack could spiral.
  • Zscaler's latest threat report notes the company blocked about 146% more attacks year over year, its biggest spike in three years, while public extortion cases rose over 70%.
  • The rise of generative AI agents adds another layer of uncertainty.
  • Ransomware is another persistent threat.
  • For Zscaler, that opens both risks and opportunities.
  • "To fight AI, you must fight it with AI," Chaudry said.
