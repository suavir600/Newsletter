Will Toll Brothers' $13.75 EPS Target Hold Amid Softer Sales Volumes?

  • In the third quarter of fiscal 2025, 81% of buyers used its incentive programs.
  • In the fiscal second quarter of 2025, gross margins slipped to 18%, down from 22.5% a year ago.
  • In the third quarter of fiscal 2025, home deliveries increased 5% from the prior year, while revenues grew 6%.
  • The company expects home closings in fiscal 2025 between 85,000 and 85,500, down from 89,690 in fiscal 2024.
  • Gross margin contracted 130 basis points (bps) year over year to 27.5%, reflecting higher incentives and product mix.
  • Net signed contracts fell 4% year over year in units, and backlog declined 19% in units and 10% in potential revenues.
  • At the same time, SG&A expenses improved 20 bps to 8.8% of revenues, supported by tighter cost control and efficiency initiatives.
