Microsoft Hikes Dividend Nearly 10% Ahead of Key Shareholder Meeting

2025-09-16T15:22:58+00:00
  • Microsoft's market worth is $3.83 trillion, and its revenue has increased by around 15% per year, thanks to its cloud and AI divisions.
  • Microsoft ( MSFT , Financials ) increased its quarterly dividend by 9.6% to $0.91 per share, the company announced Monday.
  • The future yield is around 0.71% based on Microsoft's most recent share price.
  • The prior dividend was $0.83.
  • The corporation has set the date for its annual shareholders meeting in 2025 for December 5.
  • Shareholders who own shares as of November 20 will get the dividend on December 11.
  • This article first appeared on GuruFocus .
