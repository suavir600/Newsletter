Musk’s $1 Billion Tesla Stock Buy Sends Shares Up for 2025

  • Most Read from Bloomberg Tesla’s shares jumped 6.4% at 11:44 a.m.
  • The stock is now up about 4% on a year-to-date basis, having recovered from a 45% decline as of early April.
  • The chief executive officer offloaded more than $20 billion of the company’s shares in 2022, the year he acquired Twitter.
  • Musk, 54, last bought Tesla stock in the open market in February 2020, according to data compiled by Bloomberg.
  • The purchase amounts to a show of confidence in Tesla’s prospects after a challenging first half of the year in which vehicle sales slumped 13% worldwide.
  • Tesla’s car business has continued to show signs of strain, with market researcher Cox Automotive estimating that its share of the US electric vehicle market slipped below 40% in August.
