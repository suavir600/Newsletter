Nano Banana Saves Google: Gemini Dethrones ChatGPT as GOOG Hits $3 Trillion

  • The surge helped push Alphabet's market cap past $3 trillion, with shares climbing 4% to around $252.
  • Alphabet's stock has climbed nearly 30% year-to-date, outpacing the Nasdaq's 15% gain.
  • That puts Google's parent company in rarefied air alongside Nvidia ($4.3 trillion), Microsoft ($3.8 trillion), and Apple ($3.5 trillion).
  • ChatGPT commanded 60% of AI-related web traffic with nearly 6 billion monthly visits, almost 10 times Gemini's numbers despite being integrated into Android devices and Google services used by 2 billion people.
  • Google Trends data shows global searches for Gemini overtook ChatGPT on September 12—the first time that's happened since ChatGPT's November 2022 debut.
  • Within two weeks of going viral in early September, Gemini hit #1 on both the Apple App Store and Google Play Store globally.
  • Before Nano Banana, Gemini pulled in about 13 million monthly downloads compared to ChatGPT's 64 million, according to AppMagic.
