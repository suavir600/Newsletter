Investors in Cognizant Technology Solutions (NASDAQ:CTSH) have unfortunately lost 9.8% over the last year

2025-09-15T12:16:15+00:00
  • Cognizant Technology Solutions' revenue is actually up 6.3% over the last year.
  • The share price has dropped 13% in three months.
  • That falls noticeably short of the market return of around 20%.
  • With a low yield of 1.8% we doubt that the dividend influences the share price much.
  • Longer term investors have fared much better, since the share price is up 9.4% in three years.
  • On the bright side, long term shareholders have made money, with a gain of 2% per year over half a decade.
  • During the unfortunate twelve months during which the Cognizant Technology Solutions share price fell, it actually saw its earnings per share (EPS) improve by 12%.
