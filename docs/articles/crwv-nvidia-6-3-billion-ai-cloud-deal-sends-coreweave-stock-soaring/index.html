CRWV: Nvidia $6.3 Billion AI Cloud Deal Sends Coreweave Stock Soaring

2025-09-15 21:07:10+00:00
  • Shares of CoreWeave jumped more than 5% on Monday morning, reaching $118, compared with its $40 initial public offering price earlier this year.
  • Nvidia already owns a 6.6% stake in CoreWeave and supplies its data centers with advanced GPUs, including the Blackwell line.
  • The deal, signed in April 2023 but revealed publicly this week, secures additional cloud capacity for Nvidia through April 2032.
  • Sep 15 - CoreWeave (CRWV) gained ground Monday after the AI-focused cloud provider disclosed a multi-year contract with Nvidia ( NASDAQ:NVDA ) valued at up to $6.3 billion.
  • This article first appeared on GuruFocus .
  • The new contract deepens that relationship and signals ongoing demand for AI infrastructure.
