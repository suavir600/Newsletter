Lilly's Jaypirca (pirtobrutinib), the first and only approved non-covalent (reversible) BTK inhibitor, significantly improved progression-free survival in patients with treatment-naïve CLL/SLL

  • Serious ARs occurred in 56% of patients.
  • Sepsis (6%) and febrile neutropenia (4%) occurred.
  • Mantle Cell Lymphoma Serious ARs occurred in 38% of patients.
  • The most frequent malignancy was non-melanoma skin cancer (4.6%).
  • Bleeding of any grade, excluding bruising and petechiae, occurred (17%).
  • ARs resulting in permanent discontinuation in >1% of patients included pneumonia.
  • Grade 4 decreased neutrophils (14%) and Grade 4 decreased platelets (6%) developed.
