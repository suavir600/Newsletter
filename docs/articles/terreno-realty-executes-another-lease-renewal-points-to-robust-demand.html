Terreno Realty Executes Another Lease Renewal, Points to Robust Demand

  • Its operating portfolio was 97.7% leased as of June 30, 2025.
  • TRNO’s same-store portfolio of 14.1 million square feet was 98.5% leased as of June 30, 2025.
  • The cash rents on new and renewed leases commencing during the second quarter of 2025 climbed 22.6%.
  • For the company’s improved land portfolio of 47 parcels spanning 150.6 acres, the leased rate was 95.1% as of June 30, 2025.
  • Moreover, the tenant retention ratio was 71.1% for the operating portfolio.
  • The Zacks Consensus Estimate for VICI’s 2025 FFO per share has moved a cent upward to $2.39 over the past month.
  • The Zacks Consensus Estimate for WELL’s 2025 FFO per share has moved 1 cent northward to $5.07 over the past month.
