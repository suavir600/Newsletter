Nasdaq notches record high close; traders focus on rate cuts

  • The S&P 500 is up about 10% so far in 2025, and the Nasdaq has climbed about 13%.
  • Six of the 11 S&P 500 sector indexes declined, led by utilities, down 1.07%.
  • The S&P 500 technology index rose 0.67%.
  • The S&P 500 climbed 0.21% to end at 6,495.15 points.
  • The Dow Jones Industrial Average rose 0.25% to 45,514.95 points.
  • The Nasdaq gained 0.45% to 21,798.70 points, its highest close ever.
  • Broadcom climbed 3.2%, extending its rally since the chipmaker said last Thursday it expects sharp artificial intelligence-related revenue growth.
