Tesla Could Be Worth $8.5 Trillion if Musk Hits Pay Target

  • Tesla (TSLA, Financials) may need to become an $8.5 trillion company over the next decade for Elon Musk to fully unlock his new $1 trillion compensation package, according to veteran tech analyst Gene Munster.
  • This article first appeared on GuruFocus.
  • The path to that kind of growth is far from certain.
  • Munster noted that much of Musk's pay is tied to Tesla's market value.
  • Another possibility is a deal linking Tesla with Musk's AI startup, xAI.
  • That target would make Tesla worth more than twice Nvidia today and almost eight times its current size.
  • Musk has pitched Tesla as a robotics company, with its Optimus humanoid designed to perform basic human tasks.
