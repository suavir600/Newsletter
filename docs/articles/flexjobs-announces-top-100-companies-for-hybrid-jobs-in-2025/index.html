FlexJobs Announces Top 100 Companies for Hybrid Jobs in 2025

2025-09-08 12:00:00+00:00
  • In 2025, experienced-level roles made up 68% of openings, while manager (16%) and senior manager-level jobs (9%) accounted for a quarter of postings.
  • Entry-level roles represented the remaining 7%.
  • Project management was named the leading career field for hybrid work in 2025.
  • Notably, the category outpaced computer and IT and operations, which each grew upwards of 30% to secure the number one spot.
