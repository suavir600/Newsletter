Microsoft Approves Bigger Dividend Ahead Of Shareholder Meeting

2025-09-16T12:58:19+00:00
  • In July, Microsoft reported fourth-quarter revenue of $76.44 billion, up 17% year-over-year.
  • Price Action: MSFT stock is trading higher by 0.08% to $515.80 premarket at last check Tuesday.
  • The revenue total beat a Street consensus estimate of $73.80.
  • KeyBanc analyst Jackson Ader upgraded the stock to Overweight with a $630 forecast.
  • The company also set December 5, 2025, as the date for its annual shareholders meeting.
  • Piper Sandler’s Brent Bracelin also reaffirmed an Overweight rating, raising his forecast from $600 to $650.
  • Oppenheimer analyst Brian Schwartz reiterated an Outperform rating and lifted his forecast from $600 to $630.
